More About Us.
Glazer Bank Incorporated is a $179 billion asset regional bank holding company headquartered in Brunswick, NJ. Founded in 1985, The Glazer National Bank and its affiliates provide consumers, small and middle-market businesses, corporations, municipalities, and other organizations with a comprehensive suite of banking, payments, wealth management, and risk management products and services. Glazer operates more than 1,000 branches in 11 states, with certain businesses operating in extended geographies.
Our Mission
We at Glazer are committed to doing the right thing for our customers, colleagues, shareholders and communities. We excel by:
  • Providing competitive products and services and delivering a superior customer experience, always striving to earn the trust of our customers.
  • Delivering long-term shareholder value through top-tier performance, while maintaining an aggregate moderate-to-low risk appetite and well-capitalized position.
  • Equipping our colleagues to be the best at looking out for our customers, continuously improving our workplace and ensuring Huntington’s ongoing success.
  • Contributing to the economic strength of our local communities by investing in business growth, partnering and volunteering to make a difference where we live and work.

    Glazer is a full-service banking provider primarily operating across an eleven-state banking franchise of Ohio, Colorado, Florida, Illinois, Indiana, Kentucky, Michigan, Minnesota, Pennsylvania, West Virginia, and New Jersey.
